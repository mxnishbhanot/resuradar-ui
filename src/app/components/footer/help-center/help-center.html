Help Center

Need assistance? You’ll find answers to the most common questions about using ResuRadar here.

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?

A: You can easily sign in using your Google account — no separate password required.

Q: Do I need to pay to use ResuRadar?

A: No, you can start for free! Free users can upload up to 3 resumes and access core AI analysis features.

 Resume Uploads

Q: What file types do you support?

A: Currently, we support .pdf and .docx formats.

Q: How do I compare my resume with a job description?

A: On the analysis page, you can upload both your resume and the job description. Our AI will score the match and highlight improvement areas.

 AI Analysis & ATS Score

Q: What is the ATS Score?

A: The ATS Score is an AI-based evaluation (0–10) of how well your resume aligns with modern applicant tracking systems. Higher scores indicate better structure and keyword usage.

Q: What insights will I get?

A: You’ll receive a summary report showing Your Strengths, Areas for Improvement, and a concise Executive Summary.

 Account & Premium

Q: What’s included in the Premium plan?

A: Premium users get unlimited uploads, professional-level analysis, detailed rewrite examples, recommended keywords, and portfolio enhancement tips.

Q: Can I switch from free to premium anytime?

A: Yes! You can upgrade anytime from your dashboard (coming soon).

 Technical Issues

Q: My upload failed or I got a “Network Error.” What should I do?

A: Try refreshing the page and re-uploading. If the issue persists, please contact us with the error message.

Q: The AI score didn’t generate. Why?

A: This can happen if the file is unreadable or too large. Try converting it to a clean PDF before uploading.
