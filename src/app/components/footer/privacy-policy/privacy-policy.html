Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Introduction

ResuRadar ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered resume analysis service created by Manish Kumar. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information.

What we collect

  • Account info from Google sign-in (name, email, picture)
  • Uploaded resumes and job descriptions (.pdf)
  • Usage & analytics data (IP, device, pages, timestamps)
  • Support messages you send

How we use information

We process data to provide the core service (resume analysis), authenticate users, improve product experience, and respond to support requests. We do not sell personal data.

Third-party providers

We rely on third-party services (Google Identity Services for auth, AI provider for analysis, Render for hosting, MongoDB Atlas for storage). These vendors process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it.

Security & retention

We use reasonable technical safeguards (HTTPS/TLS, access controls). We retain user data until account deletion or request, subject to operational needs.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at support@resuradar.com.
